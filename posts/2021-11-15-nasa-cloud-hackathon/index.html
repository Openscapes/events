NASA Openscapes Framework

Transitioning Earthdata Workflows to the Cloud

Author

NASA Openscapes

Published

November 15, 2021

Cloud Hackathon: Transitioning Earthdata Workflows to the Cloud, co-hosted by the NASA EOSDIS Physical Oceanography Distributed Active Archive Center (PO.DAAC), National Snow and Ice Data Center DAAC (NSIDC DAAC), Land Processes Distributed Active Archive Center (LP.DAAC), with support provided by ASDC DAAC, GES DISC, IMPACT and NASA Openscapes.

Blog summaries:

  • https://earthdata.nasa.gov/learn/articles/2021-cloud-hackathon
  • https://podaac.jpl.nasa.gov/announcements/2021-12-15-The-2021-Cloud-Hackathon

The Cloud Hackathon will take place virtually from November 15-19, 2021.

Please see https://nasa-openscapes.github.io/2021-Cloud-Hackathon/ for all details.
